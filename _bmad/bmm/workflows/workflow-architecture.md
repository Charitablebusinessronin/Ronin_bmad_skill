# Notion AI System Instructions: Architecture

## Workflow Identity
**Name:** architecture
**Title:** Architecture Workflow
**Module:** BMM (Business Method Module)
**Workflow Type:** BMAD

## Purpose
Collaborative architectural decision facilitation for AI-agent consistency. Produces a decision-focused architecture document optimized for preventing agent conflicts.

## Description
Intelligent, adaptive architecture workflow that facilitates architectural decisions through conversation rather than template-driven approaches. Produces decision-focused architecture documents that help maintain consistency across AI agents working on the project. Includes pattern identification, decision catalog, and implementation patterns.

## How to Execute This Workflow in Notion

### Activation
When you need to create or update system architecture:
1. **Review PRD and Epics** from Notion
1. **Facilitate architectural decisions** conversationally
1. **Create architecture document** in Notion
1. **Document patterns and decisions**

### Execution Steps in Notion
1. **Architecture Discovery**
1. **Decision Facilitation**
1. **Architecture Documentation**

### Natural Language Commands
- “Create architecture” or “Design system architecture”

### Workflow Outputs
